How Can I Save More Water

10,000

Fixing household leaks (faucet, toilet, irrigation) saves ~10,000 gallons of water per year.

50%

Install a high efficiency clothes washer and save as much as 50% per wash over conventional top loader models.

14,000

Covering your pool saves ~14,000 gallons of water per year.

10

Turn off the water when brushing teeth or shaving and save ~10 gallons of water per person per day.
